How to Play

Here's how the game works:

  • 🚗 Use the Left and Right arrow keys to move your car between 5 lanes.
  • 🔧 Collect correct car parts (engine, tires, battery, etc.) to fix your broken car.
  • ❌ Missing a part keeps your car broken and slower!
  • 💥 Crashing into other cars makes you lose money or parts.
  • 💸 If you run out of money or slow down too much, you lose!
  • 🏁 Fully fix your car to win and reach the finish line!
